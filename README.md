# 📣 Digital Marketing Task (Multi-Project)

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains a **Digital Marketing Task** consisting of multiple mini-projects that demonstrate different aspects of online marketing such as SEO, social media campaigns, email marketing, and analytics. Each project focuses on practical implementation of digital marketing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objectives of this task are:

* Understand different areas of digital marketing
* Apply marketing strategies in practical scenarios
* Analyze performance using real or simulated data
* Improve branding, engagement, and conversion techniques
* Build hands-on experience with marketing tools and concepts

---

## 📂 Projects Included

### 1️⃣ Social Media

* Performance tracking of social media posts
* Engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments)
* Audience reach and impressions analysis

---

### 2️⃣ SEO Optimization Report

* Keyword research and analysis
* On-page SEO improvements
* Website ranking insights

---

### 3️⃣ Email Marketing Campaign

* Email template design
* Open rate and click rate analysis
* Customer engagement tracking

---

### 4️⃣ Google Ads / Paid Campaign Analysis

* Ad performance tracking
* CPC, CTR, and ROI evaluation
* Budget optimization strategy

---

### 5️⃣ Marketing Analytics Dashboard

* Visualization of marketing data
* Performance comparison across platforms
* Insights for decision-making

---
